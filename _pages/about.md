# About me
{: #about }

Qing is an Associate Professor at the School of Computer Science, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). She received her Ph.D. from BUPT in 2022 and conducted postdoctoral research at Peking University from 2022 to 2024.Her research focuses on satellite computing, autonomous satellite operations and control, satellite task planning, emerging satellite computing applications, and resource scheduling for edge/cloud computing systems. Her work has been published in  international conferences and journals, including ACM MobiCom, USENIX ATC, USENIX Security, IEEE RTSS, IEEE TMC, IEEE TSC, and IEEE ToN. She has received several awards, including the IEEE TSC 2024 Best Paper Award,  the IEEE EDGE 2021 Best Paper Award, the ACM MobiCom 2025 Best Demo Runner-up Award; and the ICSOC 2023 Distinguished Paper Award. She has also served as a reviewer for  journals such as IEEE TMC/TSC and ACM TIST, as well as for conferences including IEEE ICDCS  and IEEE ICWS.
